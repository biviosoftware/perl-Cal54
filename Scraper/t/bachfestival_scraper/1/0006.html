Bach for Kids

Sunday, May 15, 2011, 2 p.m.

Bach for Kids is a family oriented concert performance specifically tuned to children from pre-school through elementary ages. Press Release

Through engaging participation children have exposure to Bach’s music and learn a bit about J. S. Bach, his family, and times.  The 45-minute program varies from year to year, but always incorporates a welcoming format, high quality adult or young adult musicians, and audience participation, and more.

This year’s performance is in its planning phase.  Check back on this page for details as they become available.

Bach for Kids is made possible in part through a special collaboration with the Library Concert Series at Boulder Library, Juliette Lèon Bartsch, director.  The Concert Series receives its funding from the Boulder Library Foundation.

Where:Boulder Public Library, Canyon Theater, 1001 Arapahoe Ave. (at 9th St. and Canyon)

 

Free and Open to the Public!
