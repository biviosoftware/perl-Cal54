Conor O’Neill’s Irish Pub & Restaurant

An Irish pub is the stuff of legends, just as Irish thirst is legendary. Pubs in Ireland are much more than a place for drinking, they are a social experience, part theater, part debating chamber and part livingroom. The pub often seems an extension of the living room and in many rural parts of Ireland also act as the local shop-one corner serving butter and eggs and the other serving foaming glasses of stout. Our aim at Conor O’Neill’s is to bring to Boulder a truly authentic traditional Irish Pub and Restaurant.

Please come in and enjoy great food, people, and entertainment. The dress code is casual – just relax & have a good time. Live music with rousing bands every Thursday, Friday and Saturday night. Check out our lunch specials.

Conor O’Neill’s accepts: Cash, Traveler’s Checks, Diners Club, MasterCard, Visa, and Colorado Restaurant Association Gift Certificates. Sorry, but we no longer accept American Express, Dining Dollars or Colorado Restaurant Association Certificates.
