Sara has worked at the Nelson Literary Agency since 2006. As an Associate Literary Agent\, Sara is actively acquiring new clients and enthusiastically celebrating sales for her first authors. The Nelson Literary Agency specializes in representing young adult and middle grade fiction\, romance (all genres except category and inspirational)\, science fiction and fantasy\, commercial and women’s fiction (including chick lit) and high concept literary fiction. Nelson Literary Agency is a member of AAR\, RWA\, SFWA and SCBWI. Ever wondered how a book goes from your laptop to the shelves of Boulder Book Store?  Literary agent Sara Megibow is here to tell you how the publishing process works. She’ll also give tips on what agents are looking for\, how to query an agent\, how to pitch your book\, and how essential those first few pages really are. We’ll end the afternoon with a Q&\;A\, so bring questions!

Join us for an afternoon with local Boulder authors Cara Lopez Lee\, Carolyn Jones\, Kathy Guyton and Kristen Moeller! 

Cara Lopez Lee\, author of They Only Eat Their Husbands. “A delightfully engaging read. Lee reminds us that in travel as in life\, the inward journey is as messy\, important and rewarding as the outward one.”
—Kyle Wagner\, travel editor\, The Denver Post

Carolyn Jones\, author of Opening the Gates of the Heart. This is a book that speaks to those on a journey of self-awareness\, healing\, and awakening. Through up-lifting prose and photographs\, award-winning author and photographer Carolyn Jones shares with us her own journey of emotional and spiritual healing\, from feelings of worthlessness and deep despair\, to joy and peace.

Kathy Guyton\, author of U.S. State Names.  "\;Whether you have a specific state in mind or want to know a little bit about all of them\, this is a fun book to read. Even the states you think are fairly obvious (like Virginia) there's more to the story than you thought. My target states were Arizona and California (and both were interesting to read!) but I have enjoyed randomly reading other chapters."\;
—Liza Weissler

Kristen Moeller\, author of Waiting for Jack. “By the end of this book and your personal exploration\, you will see where you wait for life to begin. You will see that all the answers you need come from within. You will learn to stop hiding your magnificence and stop jacking around. You will become\, as Kristen proclaims\, 'a fierce disruption of the ordinary.'"\;
—Jack Canfield\, Co-author\, Chicken Soup for the Soul
